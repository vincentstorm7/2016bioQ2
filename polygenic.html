How it Works

If variety is the spice of life, polygenic inheritance is one genetic mechanism giving us a continuous range of possibilities.

The determination of a particular characteristic, e.g. skin colour, by many genes (polygenes), each having a small effect individually. Characteristics controlled in this way show continuous variation.
